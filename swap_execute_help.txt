One-shot swap: quote ΓåÆ approve (if needed) ΓåÆ swap ΓåÆ sign & broadcast ΓåÆ txHash

Usage: onchainos.exe swap execute [OPTIONS] --from <FROM> --to <TO> --amount <AMOUNT> --chain <CHAIN> --wallet <WALLET>

Options:
      --base-url <BASE_URL>
          Backend service URL (overrides config)
      --from <FROM>
          Source token contract address
      --to <TO>
          Destination token contract address
      --amount <AMOUNT>
          Amount in minimal units (wei/lamports)
      --chain <CHAIN>
          Chain (e.g. ethereum, solana, xlayer)
      --wallet <WALLET>
          User wallet address
      --slippage <SLIPPAGE>
          Slippage tolerance in percent. Omit to use autoSlippage
      --gas-level <GAS_LEVEL>
          Gas priority: slow, average, fast [default: average]
      --swap-mode <SWAP_MODE>
          Swap mode: exactIn or exactOut [default: exactIn]
      --tips <TIPS>
          Jito tips in SOL for Solana MEV protection
      --max-auto-slippage <MAX_AUTO_SLIPPAGE>
          Max auto slippage percent cap
      --mev-protection
          Enable MEV protection
  -h, --help
          Print help
